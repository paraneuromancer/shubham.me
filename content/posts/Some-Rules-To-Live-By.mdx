Below I a list I have compiled over the years of things I have found to be true and useful in life. 

1.  You wake up early, even when no one's watching.
2.  You train your body instead of punishing it.
3.  You do hard things dailly, on purpose.
4.  You keep promises to yourself, especially the small ones.
5.  You sit in silence instead of reaching out to your mobile.
6.  You move even on days you dont feel like it.
7.  You Eat for function.
8.  You Choose fewer people and deeper bonds.
9.  You say no without over explaining.
10. You breath slow when life feels fast.
11. You face discomfornt, instead of numbing it.
12. You stop waiting for motivation and take action anyway.
13. Youy spend time outdoors daily, it increases the causality. 
14. You Write down what you've been avoiding.
15. You stop comparing and start tracking 
16. You build something that takes time.
17. You train focus not just muscles.
18. You take resposibility, without beating yourself up.
19. Reduce dopamine hits and excess stimulation.
20. Compete only with yourself.
21. Let go versions of you that no longer serve you.
22. Laugh, play and allow lightness back in.
23. You show up consistently, not perfectly.
24. You choose self-respect over short-term comfort
25. You prioritize sleep like it's a performance enhancer.
26. You practice gratitude for what is, not just what could be.
27. You forgive yourself and others, because holding on is heavy.
28. You learn to be alone without being lonely.
29. You seek understanding before seeking agreement.
30. You build systems, not just goals.
31. You embrace discomfort as a sign of growth.
32. You celebrate small wins like they're Olympic medals.
33. You rest without guilt.
34. You ask for help when you need it.
35. You give help without expecting anything in return.
36. You stay curious.
37. You keep learning.
38. You stay humble.
39. You stay kind.
40. You stay true to yourself.
